Oat Recipe

Oats are one of the best foods for your health that exist on this planet. It has a nice texture and the warmth makes you feel comfort in any time of day.

Ingredients:

  • Milk
  • Oats

Steps:

  1. Add oats to bowl
  2. Add milk to bowl so that it is about a quarter centimetre over the oats.
  3. Microwave for 1 minute 30 seconds
  4. Take out stir
  5. Put back in for 1 minute
  6. Let it cool for 1 min, then enjoy!
